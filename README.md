Keyboard Oscilloscope — Day 07
Turn your keyboard into a synthesizer and watch the physics happen in real time.
Every key press generates a sine wave at a musical frequency. Hold multiple keys and you get a chord — the waves add together (superposition) just like in a real speaker or analog synthesizer. 
A live oscilloscope display shows each individual wave and the combined signal simultaneously.

What it looks like

Top panel — one channel per held key, each at its own frequency
Bottom panel — the superposed (summed) waveform, exactly what your speaker is playing
Press more keys → watch the combined wave get more complex
